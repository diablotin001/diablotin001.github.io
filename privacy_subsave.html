Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

Welcome to SubSave. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with subscription tracking and financial management features, we may process the following data:

  • Subscription Data: Information about your subscriptions, billing cycles, and costs that you manually enter.
  • Purchase History: Information regarding your in-app purchases and subscription status.
  • Technical Data: Anonymous device information such as OS version and device model for troubleshooting.

2. Third-Party Services

We do not operate our own servers for personal data storage. We rely on the following trusted partners to provide app functionality:

RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and verify your premium status. RevenueCat receives anonymized transaction identifiers and receipt data. They do not have access to your name, credit card number, or specific identity. You can view their policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Apple iCloud: To sync your data across your devices, your data is stored in Apple’s iCloud. We (the developers) do not have access to your private iCloud database. Your data is protected by Apple's security and privacy infrastructure.

3. Data Security

We prioritize your data security:

  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account with us. Your identity is managed through your Apple ID.
  • Local Storage: Most data is stored locally on your device's secure sandbox and only leaves the device via iCloud encryption.

4. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Data Deletion: You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app or clearing your iCloud data in your device settings.
  • Subscription Management: You can manage or cancel your subscriptions at any time through your App Store Account Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
